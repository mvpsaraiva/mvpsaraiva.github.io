CityCell

Urban growth simulation model based on cellular automata, enabling users to explore urban growth dynamics and its interaction with the natural environment via a variety of models available. Built with Embarcadero Delphi.

More Information

CityCell can be downloaded from its website, mantained by the Laboratory of Urbanism of the Federal University of Pelotas.
